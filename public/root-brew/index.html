ROOTBREW
High-fidelity design of an app concept.
ROLE
Concept, Design
TOOLS
Sketch
DATE
Fall 2017
RootBrew is an on-demand, customizable ginger beer brewing service. The app offers the user the ability to customize, order, manage, and track their brews remotely.
This was a 5-week exercise for a UX + web design course. By relying heavily on graphics over text, I designed this app to make ordering and monitoring brews as simple and enjoyable as possible for the user.
